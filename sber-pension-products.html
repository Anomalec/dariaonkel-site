Pension Products Experience

Designing digital product experiences for long-term savings and pension planning, where clarity and confidence matter more than speed alone.

Role

Product design for complex financial products with a strong information-design component.

Domain

Pension products, savings logic, and longer-horizon financial decision-making.

Focus

Making difficult scenarios easier to compare, understand, and act on.

Financial planning Long-term products made easier to compare

A product direction centered on pension plans, savings logic, and clearer decision support.

Context

Pension products are often difficult to understand because they combine financial logic, long time horizons, and emotionally significant decisions into one experience.

The design work focused on making those journeys clearer without flattening the complexity users actually need to understand.

What I worked on

  • Improved structure and presentation of long-term financial product information.
  • Reduced friction in comparison and decision-support scenarios.
  • Helped shape interfaces that support trust and comprehension over time.
  • Designed for users making non-routine, high-consideration financial choices.
Design goal Help users feel more informed and less overwhelmed when interacting with long-term savings and pension products.

Outcome

The work contributed to clearer decision pathways and better communication of complex product logic in a domain where trust and understanding are especially important.
