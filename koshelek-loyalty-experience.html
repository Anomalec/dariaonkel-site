Loyalty Experience

Designing loyalty mechanics, promotional touchpoints, and repeat-use scenarios that support stronger engagement over time.

Role

Product design for retention-oriented flows, merchant interactions, and offer presentation.

Domain

Loyalty, cashback, offers, and recurring value within a consumer product environment.

Focus

Engagement, repeat interaction, and balancing promotional value with product clarity.

Retention design Loyalty journeys built around repeat interaction

A case centered on cashback, offers, merchant touchpoints, and repeat engagement patterns.

Context

Loyalty products need to do more than present offers: they need to create reasons to return, make value easy to notice, and keep promotional mechanics understandable.

The design work focused on building those repeated engagement moments into a more coherent product experience.

What I worked on

  • Designed merchant-facing and user-facing loyalty touchpoints.
  • Worked on offer presentation, promotional visibility, and cashback-related interactions.
  • Improved how users discover, understand, and return to loyalty value.
  • Supported product retention goals through clearer interaction design.
Design goal Make loyalty value feel visible, timely, and worth returning for without overwhelming the user with promotional noise.

Outcome

The resulting direction supported more consistent engagement patterns and better visibility of merchant and loyalty mechanics across the product experience.
